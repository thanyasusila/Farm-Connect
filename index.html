How FarmConnect Works

Connecting soil and spoon in three simple steps

01

Farmers Register

Farmers list their fresh harvests, upload descriptions, locations, and set fair prices per kilogram.

02

Consumers Shop

Customers browse the shop by categories (Vegetables, Fruits, Grains, Organic) and place direct orders.

03

Direct Delivery

Produce is packed at the farm and delivered directly to the consumer's doorstep, retaining full freshness.

Empowering both ends of the chain

Why choose direct-from-farm connection?

For Farmers

100% Middlemen Free

Keep 100% of the sales revenue and set your own agricultural crop prices.

Direct Market Access

Reach thousands of consumers in cities without travel or wholesale agents.

Secure & Fast Payments

Get money transferred directly to your bank account upon delivery validation.

For Consumers

Nutrient-Rich Freshness

Receive produce harvested hours ago instead of items stored in cold warehouses.

Traceability to the Source

Know exactly which farm, village, and farmer grew the food your family eats.

Fair, Honest Pricing

Slightly lower costs than grocery stores, while paying farmers much higher rates.
